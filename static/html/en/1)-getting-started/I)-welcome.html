Getting Started

This Getting Started Guide is for partners that are new to LabTech®. This guide will walk you through the process of installing and configuring LabTech® Ignite ® to suit your business needs. There are hundreds of properties that change the behavior of patching, reporting, sensor monitors, etc. Due to the volume of properties, the LabTech® Ignite ® plugin was created to make it easier to configure your system quickly and is included in new installations.
